COVID-19 Chatbot

Website Integration

This JSFiddle demonstrates the integration of the COVID-19 Chatbot powered by Symptoma into a website.

The chatbot will ask questions about symptoms
leading to a risk level for the Coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19).

➽ Get the JavaScript code from
www.symptoma.com/covid-19
and insert it into a <script>-tag
of your website!
